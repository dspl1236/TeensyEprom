POWER FIRST: Bus bars before any signal wires. Tap 5V and GND to Teensy, measure 3.3V on Teensy 3V3 pad before proceeding.

PIN 13 SKIP: Teensy pin 13 = onboard LED. A3 → pin 14. A7 → pin 36 (bottom of board).

ADDR HIGH ROUTING: These wires drop below the GND bus bar into the bottom channel (8 stacked lanes). Each wire has its own lane — use the colour to trace it back to the correct DIP pin.
