WIRE COUNT: Total wires soldered = 11 power/GND branches + 16 socket→chip + 16 chip→Teensy (data) + 16 socket→chip + 16 chip→Teensy (addr low+high) + 2 control (/OE divider) + 1 DIR = approximately 60 individual solder joints. Work in groups, test continuity after each group before moving to the next.
